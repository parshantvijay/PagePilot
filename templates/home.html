How Our Recommender Engine Works

Our recommender engine leverages Collaborative Item Based Filtering, a sophisticated technique that analyzes user interactions to generate personalized book recommendations.

Collaborative Item Based Filtering - Recommender System

Collaborative Item Based Filtering is at the heart of our recommendation system. It's a process that revolves around understanding user preferences and recommending books based on similar user interactions. Here's a detailed overview:

  1. Identification of High Rating Users
  2. Filtering Ratings
  3. Selection of Popular Books by High-Rated Users
  4. Creation of a Pivot Table
  5. Calculation of Similarity Scores
  6. Recommendation Generation

The data powering our recommender engine is sourced from Kaggle's Book Recommendation Dataset, ensuring the integrity and richness of our recommendation system.
